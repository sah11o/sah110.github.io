About

Hi!

This is my personal website, where I am not sure what I am going to do (blogs? research follow-alongs?). I am a third year undergraduate at IIT Kharagpur. My research interests lie in the fields of Deep Learning, Natural Language Processing, Image Processing, Reinforcement Learning and Usable Security and Privacy.

My learning methodology in the above fields has always been engaging in projects and to pick up the required core concepts on-the-go. I love to work on projects that create a meaningful impact using concepts I learn everyday. This helps me shape my knowledge in these concepts in a more practical and application based approach, which makes the learning fun!

Other than the “research business”, I love film making and photography. I use social media platforms like Instagram (my personal account currently) to express myself in these art forms. I plan on starting up a separate page to publish my work soon!

That’s about it.
